# SWG (Strategic Wealth Group)

AI transformation roadmap for a wealth management and tax firm.

- **Timeline:** 2026-01-01 through 2026-12-31

## Products Being Built

- **Tax One-Pagers** — communicating wealth management insights to tax clients, helping prepare for tax season
- **RMD Agent** — organizing and preparing Required Minimum Distributions for clients age 73+
- **Unified Chat Experience** — single chat interface across all their data sources:
  - QuickBooks
  - SharePoint
  - Wealthbox (CRM)
  - LPL client data

## Operational AI

- Building reports
- Creating processes and documentation
- Implementing AI efficiency across wealth management, tax group, and back-end admin work

## Stack

- React/Vite frontend
