# Sprint Process

## Sprint Cadence

- **Weekly sprints** (Monday to Friday)
- **Scrum Master:** Jake Shumaker
- **Assistant Scrum Master:** Walter Miller
- **Participants:** Entire team currently; will narrow to engineering team as Pulse grows

## Ceremonies

### Monday — Sprint Planning
- Clean up the active board in ClickUp
- Score tasks based on what's complete from previous sprint
- Ensure the team is set to start the week

### Daily — Standup (15 min, mornings)
- Every weekday

### Every Other Friday — Sprint Retro
- Covers the past two sprints
- Format: positives, negatives, goals for next two sprints
